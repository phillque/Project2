Learning Quentins 5 Step Spaghetti Recipe
Sunday night dinner: Authentic Pasta Recipe:

STEP 1 Prepare the Pasta


Pastas Aiming at Boiling Water in Pot by Jeshoots pexels

Boil Baby Boil

  • Grab Medium size pot
  • Bring water to a boil
  • Add raw pasta

STEP 2 Prepare ground beef


Fresh Picadillo in Pan Left on Colorful Tea Towel by Keesha's Kitchen pexels

The Greatest ground beef simmers in seasoning

  • Grab medium sizesautee pan
  • Add ground beef
  • constantly mix meat in the pan

STEP 3 Prepare tomato sauce


Cherry Tomatoes on a Frying Pan By Filipp Romanovski pexels

Fresh tomatos make for a better dish

  • Add tomato sauce in sauce pan
  • Turn heat to medium low
  • Season sauce and stir continuosly

STEP 4 Combine Pasta, ground beef & tomato sauce


Combining Everthing together by cottonbro studio pexels

Perfectly cooked pasta makes a huge difference

  • Add Meat to the sauce
  • Strain the noodles
  • Add noodles to Meat and Tomato sauce

STEP 5 Garnish the Spaghetti


Garnishing your dish by cottonbro studio Pexels

The perfect Garnish makes the Pasta easier to eat

  • Add cheese
  • Add mint leaves
  • Serve with a smile
